# 🏨 Google Hotels: Global Accommodation & Hospitality Intelligence

This module is a high-performance data extraction solution designed for the Google Hotels ecosystem. It enables travel agencies, hospitality analysts, and booking platforms to retrieve real-time data on hotel availability, pricing, and guest sentiments.

### 🚀 Hospitality Extraction Capabilities:

#### 1. 🏨 Comprehensive Hotel Intelligence
* **Core Property Data:** Automated extraction of basic hotel information, including property names, locations, and contact details.
* **Pricing & Availability:** Real-time monitoring of room rates and availability across different booking dates.
* **Accommodation Types:** Captures detailed data on various room types to support granular comparison.

#### 2. 🌟 Rating & Reputation Analysis
* **Guest Ratings:** Extracts overall ratings and specific review metrics to assess property quality.
* **Hospitality Benchmarking:** Ideal for analyzing competitive positioning within the local or global hotel market.

#### 3. 🗺️ Integration for Travel Platforms
* **Booking Engine Support:** Provides the structured data foundation needed for hotel booking platforms and accommodation search engines.
* **Recommendation Systems:** Supplies hotel details and price points to power intelligent travel recommendation algorithms.

### 🛠️ Technical Advantage
- **Granular Detail Extraction:** Specifically engineered to parse complex hotel listings, ensuring all basic information and specific hotel details are captured accurately.
- **Structured Hospitality Feeds:** Delivers all hotel metadata in clean, developer-friendly formats for rapid deployment in travel apps.
- **Scalable Market Monitoring:** Built to support large-scale tracking of hotel inventory across multiple geographical regions.
