# 🛍️ Google Product: E-commerce Data Mining & User Profiling

This module is a high-performance solution designed to automatically crawl Google product search results and related recommendations. It is engineered to support deep e-commerce data mining and potential interested group analysis for targeted marketing.

### 🚀 Product Extraction Capabilities:

#### 1. 🔍 Detailed Product Intelligence
* **Product Results:** Automated extraction of product listings, specifications, and core details.
* **Market Monitoring:** Ideal for tracking product changes, new specifications, and category-level shifts.
* **Potential Interests:** Identifies related products and potential interest groups for strategic user profiling.

#### 2. 🗣️ Review & Specification Analysis
* **Product Reviews:** Scrapes full user reviews to facilitate sentiment analysis and product evaluation.
* **Technical Specs:** Captures granular product specifications for technical benchmarking.
