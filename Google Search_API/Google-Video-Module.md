# 🎥 Google Search: Video & Multimedia Intelligence

This module specializes in the automated extraction of video content and metadata from Google Search results. It is designed to capture everything from highlighted long-form videos to the increasingly popular "Short Video" segments across various platforms.

### 🚀 Video Extraction Capabilities:

#### 1. General & Highlighted Videos
* **Inline Video Analysis:** Extracts video titles, durations, and original source platforms (e.g., YouTube, Vimeo).
* **Highlighted Video Boxes:** Captures featured video snippets that appear at the top of the search results for specific tutorials or queries.
* **Thumbnail & Preview:** Scrapes high-quality video thumbnails and preview metadata for visual cataloging.

#### 2. Short Video Intelligence
* **Trending Shorts:** Specifically engineered to extract data from Google's "Short Video" shelf, including content from YouTube Shorts and other integrated platforms.
* **Trend Monitoring:** Ideal for tracking viral content, social trends, and platform-specific video performance.

#### 3. Metadata & Contextual Data
* **Source Attribution:** Identifies the hosting platform and the original uploader/channel information.
* **Timestamp & Freshness:** Tracks when the video was published to monitor the latest media trends.
* **Rich Descriptions:** Extracts associated video descriptions and snippet text for keyword analysis.

### 🛠️ Technical Specifications
- **Multi-Platform Coverage:** Captures video data regardless of the hosting site (YouTube, News sites, Social Media).
- **Automated Scalability:** Built to handle thousands of video-related keywords with high-concurrency support.
- **Precision Output:** Delivers structured video data in JSON or CSV formats, ready for analysis or media archiving.
