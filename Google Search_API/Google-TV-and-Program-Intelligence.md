# 📺 Google Search: TV Channels & Program Intelligence

This module is designed to extract real-time television schedules, program categories, and broadcasting metadata directly from Google’s media widgets. It provides a structured view of what’s airing across various networks and streaming platforms.

### 🚀 Media Extraction Capabilities:

#### 1. 📺 TV Program Categories
* **Genre Classification:** Extracts program types such as News, Reality TV, Drama, Sports, and Movies.
* **Show Metadata:** Scrapes show titles, descriptions, and season/episode information where available.
* **Channel Identification:** Captures the names of the broadcasting networks and channels.

#### 2. 📅 Broadcasting Schedules
* **Air Times:** Real-time extraction of "What's on Now" and upcoming show schedules.
* **Platform Availability:** Identifies which streaming services or cable providers are airing specific programs.
* **Regional Listings:** Supports localized TV guides based on geographic search parameters.

#### 3. 🎬 Multimedia & Ratings
* **Program Thumbnails:** Extracts poster art and promotional images for TV shows and movies.
* **Viewer Sentiment:** Captures star ratings and audience reviews for specific programs or channels.
* **Recommendation Lists:** Scrapes "Similar Shows" or "Top Rated Programs" suggested by Google.

### 🛠️ Technical Advantage
- **Real-Time Updates:** Optimized to capture live broadcasting data which changes hourly.
- **Structured Media Feed:** Converts visual TV guides into clean, machine-readable JSON/CSV formats.
- **Global Coverage:** Ability to pull international TV listings and program metadata in multiple languages.
