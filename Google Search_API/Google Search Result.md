# 🔍 Google Search: General & Advertising Intelligence

This module provides high-precision data extraction from Google’s primary search engine results pages (SERP). It is engineered to capture everything from organic rankings to complex advertising layouts and rich data snippets.

### 🚀 Key Extraction Capabilities:

#### 1. Organic Search Intelligence
* **General Results:** Full extraction of page titles, destination URLs, and meta-snippets.
* **Additional Hyperlinks:** Captures sitelinks and sub-menu links for deeper site mapping.
* **Rank Tracking:** Precise positioning of organic listings within the global SERP.

#### 2. Advertising & Commercial Data
* **Ad Content Analysis:** Full details on sponsored listings including Ad Titles, Store Names, and destination links.
* **Pricing Intelligence:** Real-time extraction of product prices directly from search ads.
* **Visual Assets:** Scrapes ad thumbnails and promotional images for creative monitoring.

#### 3. Rich Snippets & Knowledge Extraction
* **Information Boxes:** Extracts data from General Answer boxes, Map boxes, and Knowledge categories.
* **Numerical & Data Lists:** Structured capture of enumeration categories and source explanations.
* **Interactive Elements:** Analysis of Event titles, Location categories, and Food/Store category listings.

#### 4. Ratings & Product Metrics
* **Social Proof:** Extraction of Rating categories (stars) and review counts.
* **Product Insights:** Captures specific product categories and pinned item highlights for e-commerce analysis.

### 🛠️ Technical Advantage
* **Multi-threaded Concurrency:** Designed for high-speed, batch processing of massive keyword lists.
* **Anti-Bot Resilience:** Built-in CAPTCHA recognition and automated failure retry logic.
* **Structured Output:** Delivers data in ready-to-use JSON or CSV formats.
