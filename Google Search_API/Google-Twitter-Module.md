# 🐦 Twitter (X) Data & Social Intelligence Module

This module is engineered for high-speed extraction of real-time data from Twitter (X). It allows businesses to monitor social trends, track brand sentiment, and archive public conversations at scale.

### 🚀 Twitter Extraction Capabilities:

#### 1. 📊 Profile & User Intelligence
* **User Metadata:** Extraction of profile bios, follower/following counts, verification status, and account creation dates.
* **Profile Media:** Scrapes profile pictures, banner images, and linked URLs.
* **Activity Tracking:** Monitors post frequency and interaction patterns of specific accounts.

#### 2. 📝 Tweet & Content Analysis
* **Thread Extraction:** Full capture of original tweets and their subsequent replies for conversation mapping.
* **Multimedia Content:** Scrapes images, videos, and GIFs attached to tweets with high-resolution links.
* **Engagement Metrics:** Real-time extraction of Like counts, Retweets, Quotes, and View counts.

#### 3. 🔍 Trend & Keyword Monitoring
* **Hashtag Analysis:** Tracks trending hashtags and the volume of discussion around specific topics.
* **Keyword Search:** Bulk scraping of tweets based on specific keywords, phrases, or advanced search operators.
* **Global Sentiment:** Ideal for market research and identifying viral social movements.

### 🛠️ Technical Advantage
- **Anti-Rate Limit Tech:** Optimized to handle Twitter's strict rate limits for continuous data flow.
- **Dynamic Content Scraping:** Specialized in capturing JavaScript-rendered content and infinite scroll feeds.
- **Structured Delivery:** Outputs data in clean JSON or CSV formats for seamless integration into social listening dashboards.
