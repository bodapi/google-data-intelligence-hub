# 🧠 Google Search: Knowledge & People Intelligence

This module provides high-precision extraction of structured data from Google’s Knowledge Graph. It is designed to capture comprehensive information about public figures, geographical locations, and categorized entities directly from the search results.

### 🚀 Knowledge Extraction Capabilities:

#### 1. 👤 People & Personality Insights
* **People Categories:** Automated extraction of profiles for public figures, celebrities, and professionals.
* **Biographical Data:** Captures key details such as profession, notable works, and social connections.
* **Social Proof:** Extracts associated ratings, reviews, and public sentiment metrics where applicable.

#### 2. 🏛️ Location & Entity Intelligence
* **Location Categories:** Structured data extraction for landmarks, cities, and points of interest.
* **Geographic Metadata:** Captures essential location information, including descriptions, coordinates, and regional context.
* **Knowledge Panels:** Full extraction of the "Knowledge Category" boxes that appear on the right side of the SERP.

#### 3. 📚 Structured Fact Extraction
* **Entity Attributes:** Identifies and scrapes specific attributes belonging to a search entity (e.g., height, birth date, or headquarters).
* **Source Attribution:** Tracks the origin of the knowledge snippets to ensure data transparency and authority.
* **Related Entities:** Scrapes "People Also Search For" lists to map out connections between different topics or figures.

### 🛠️ Technical Advantage
- **Deep Entity Mapping:** Goes beyond simple text scraping to understand the relationship between different knowledge nodes.
- **High Concurrency:** Optimized for bulk research on thousands of entities or locations simultaneously.
- **Clean Data Feed:** Delivers complex knowledge structures in simplified JSON or CSV formats for easy database ingestion.
