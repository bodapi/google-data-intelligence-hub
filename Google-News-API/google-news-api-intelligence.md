# 📰 Google News API: Media & Topic Intelligence

This module is a specialized data extraction solution for the Google News ecosystem. It is engineered to crawl structured news lists, related topics, and publication metadata for use in news aggregation, media analysis, and trend tracking platforms.

### 🚀 News Extraction Capabilities:

#### 1. 🗞️ Structured News Aggregation
* **News Results:** Automated extraction of news headlines, article snippets, and direct publication links.
* **Related Topics:** Identifies trending themes and associated news clusters for deep topical research.
* **Publication Information:** Captures metadata regarding the news source, publisher authority, and timestamps.

#### 2. 🔍 Navigation & Media Analysis
* **Column & Menu Links:** Scrapes menu, submenu, and column-specific links for comprehensive site mapping.
* **Relevant Publications:** Identifies authoritative sources and linked media outlets for specific news cycles.
* **Topic Tracking:** Ideal for monitoring the evolution of public opinion or industry-specific news over time.

### 🛠️ Technical Advantage
- **High-Fidelity Crawling:** Optimized to handle complex news grid layouts and dynamic content feeds.
- **Analytical Readiness:** Delivers clean, structured data perfect for sentiment analysis and media monitoring dashboards.
