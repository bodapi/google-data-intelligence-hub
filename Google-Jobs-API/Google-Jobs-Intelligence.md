# 💼 Google Jobs: Recruitment & Market Intelligence Module

This module is a specialized data extraction solution designed to scrape job listings directly from Google’s Job search results. It enables HR tech platforms, recruitment agencies, and market analysts to integrate real-time employment data and analyze labor market trends.

### 🚀 Job Extraction Capabilities:

#### 1. 🔍 Comprehensive Job Listings
* **Job Core Details:** Automated extraction of job titles, company names, and detailed work locations.
* **Employment Types:** Identifies job classifications such as Full-time, Part-time, Contract, or Internship.
* **Salary Insights:** Captures salary ranges and compensation data where available in the search snippets.

#### 2. ⏳ Posting & Freshness Metadata
* **Time Tracking:** Scrapes "posted time" (e.g., 2 days ago, 5 hours ago) to ensure data freshness and urgency.
* **Source Attribution:** Identifies the original recruitment website or company portal where the job was posted.
* **Market Recency:** Ideal for monitoring the latest openings in specific industries or geographic regions.

#### 3. 🛠️ Advanced Filtering & Integration
* **Filtering Criteria:** Supports extraction based on specific job search filters used in the Google Jobs interface.
* **Market Analysis:** Provides structured data for analyzing hiring demand, company growth, and regional job availability.
* **Seamless Integration:** Delivers parsed data in clean JSON/CSV formats, ready for recruitment CRM or job board ingestion.

### 🛠️ Technical Advantage
- **Structured Parsing:** Converts complex job search UI elements into high-fidelity data points.
- **Scalable Recruitment Tech:** Designed to handle bulk queries across thousands of job titles and industries.
- **Enterprise-Grade Accuracy:** Specifically tuned to capture the key fields required for professional job board synchronization.
