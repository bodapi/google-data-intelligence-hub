# 📜 Google Patent Details: In-Depth Intellectual Property Intelligence

This module is a specialized data extraction solution designed for deep-level patent research. It is engineered to capture complete, granular information about a single patent to support advanced patent mining, technology trend research, and legal analysis.

### 🚀 Deep Extraction Capabilities:

#### 1. 🔍 Comprehensive Patent Metadata
* **Core Identification:** Automated extraction of patent titles, unique patent numbers, and the country of origin.
* **Inventor & Ownership:** Scrapes detailed information about inventors and assignees to track innovation ownership.
* **Critical Timelines:** Captures precise application and publication dates for historical and trend analysis.

#### 2. 📄 Technical & Legal Depth
* **Full Abstracts & Descriptions:** Retrieves complete technical abstracts and detailed descriptions for deep content evaluation.
* **Claims & Classifications:** Extracts formal patent claims and technical classifications to define the scope of the intellectual property.
* **Legal Status:** Captures the current legal standing of the patent to support due diligence and risk assessment.

#### 3. 🕸️ Relational & Citation Mapping
* **Citation Relationships:** Maps out citation links to identify foundational technologies and related technical advancements.
* **Extended Resources:** Scrapes related links and external references for more comprehensive technical research.
* **Patent Mining:** Provides the data foundation required for building complex technology trend models.

### 🛠️ Technical Advantage
- **Single-Entity Granularity:** Optimized to pull every available data point for a specific patent record, ensuring no technical detail is missed.
- **Structured Legal Feeds:** Delivers complex legal and technical data in clean, structured JSON formats for seamless integration into research platforms.
- **Global Intelligence Support:** Designed to handle diverse patent documentation styles across different international jurisdictions.
